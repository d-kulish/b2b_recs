Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (email address, name) and data you upload to the platform for model training purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide the Service, including:

  • Account authentication and management
  • Processing your data through ETL pipelines
  • Training and deploying ML models on your behalf
  • Service improvement and technical support

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure in the customer's isolated GCP project. The default region is europe-central2 (Warsaw, Poland) unless a different region is selected during onboarding. We use encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only. Recs Studio does not copy customer data outside the customer's project.

4. Data Ownership

You retain full ownership of all data you upload to the platform. We do not use your data for any purpose other than providing the Service to you.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following subprocessors to process your data:

  • Google Cloud Platform — BigQuery, Cloud Storage, Cloud Run, Vertex AI, Cloud SQL, Cloud Scheduler, Secret Manager (infrastructure and ML services)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery only

Your use of Google Cloud Platform services is subject to Google Cloud's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may export your data at any time through the platform's API.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management and authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the Service.

10. Data Processing Addendum

A Data Processing Addendum (DPA) incorporating the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) is available upon request for customers subject to GDPR or other data protection regulations. To request a DPA, contact us at support@recs.studio.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@recs.studio.
